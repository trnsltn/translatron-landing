Our Philosophy

The Style Guide

A good translation is not just about words, it's about the experience. We think a good translation should be consistent, natural, beautiful, and must fit perfectly.

1. Consistent

The same things need to have the same names everywhere. Not just within one app, but also on all platforms.

2. Natural

You don't have to always stick to the English original. Sometimes it makes more sense to simply describe what the app does.

3. Default

Users have seen many apps. It's easier for them if familiar concepts have familiar names. Look at what Apple, Google, and others use.

4. Beautiful

Use good language that would make the app look as if it was built in your region. Respect your language's grammar and style.

5. And it MUST FIT

This last one is never easy. Text must fit into its allotted space. Sometimes you need to change surrounding phrases to make it work.

6. Typographically Sound

Text is design. Avoid hanging words, gaps, and awkward line breaks. Ensure it works on all screen sizes and zoom levels.
